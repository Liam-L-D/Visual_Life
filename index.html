Exploring My Journey in the US

Hello everyone! My name is Lian Duan. This project is a visual representation of my life journey, capturing the significant milestones and transformations that have shaped who I am today. It begins in 2013, when I made the life-changing move from Yueyang, Hunan, China, to Albany, New York, in the United States. This transition marked the start of an incredible journey filled with challenges, growth, and self-discovery.

As I settled into my new environment, I embarked on an educational path that led me through various stages of personal and professional development. Starting with high school in Albany, I progressed to community college, where I began exploring my interests in art and design. Initially pursuing fine art, I found my passion evolving toward graphic design, which eventually led me to the interdisciplinary field of Information Design and Technology. This transformation reflects not only a shift in my academic focus but also my growing desire to merge creativity with technology to solve complex problems and communicate effectively.

Throughout my academic journey, I experienced numerous pivotal moments, including earning my undergraduate degree and advancing to a master's program. Along the way, I encountered diverse cultures, built meaningful relationships, and adapted to new challenges. One of the most significant events during this time was meeting my wife, whose support and companionship have enriched my life profoundly. From our first meeting to our marriage, this personal milestone represents the culmination of shared dreams and mutual growth.

This project not only chronicles my geographical, educational, and personal transitions but also serves as a reflection of my aspirations and resilience. Through visualizations and storytelling, it offers a unique glimpse into the path I have traveled from a small city in China to building a life and family in the United States. It is a celebration of growth, love, and the pursuit of dreams, inviting you to explore the intricate connections and defining moments that have brought me to where I am today.

Thank you for taking the time to explore my project!
