Exploring My Journey in the US

Hello everyone! My name is Liam L. Duan. This project is a visual representation of my life journey, capturing the significant milestones and transformations that have shaped who I am today.

Moving to the United States opened a new chapter in my life. As I adapted to a different environment, I also began a path of growth through education, creativity, and personal experience.

Introduction

As I settled into my new environment, I embarked on an educational path that led me through various stages of personal and professional development.

Throughout my academic journey, I experienced numerous pivotal moments, including earning my undergraduate degree and advancing to a master’s program.

This project not only chronicles my journey, but also reflects my aspirations, resilience, and the lessons I have gained over time.

What This Website Includes

Each page on this website highlights a different part of my story. Together, they present a broader picture of my experiences, academic background, and personal growth.

The My Journey page introduces important experiences and transitions in my life. The Timeline page presents major milestones in chronological order. The My Major page explains my academic interests and how they connect to my future goals.

Reflection

Looking back, my journey has been shaped by change, persistence, and the desire to keep learning. Every milestone represents not only an achievement, but also a step toward becoming the person I am today.

I hope this website offers a clear and meaningful overview of my story and the path I continue to build for the future.
